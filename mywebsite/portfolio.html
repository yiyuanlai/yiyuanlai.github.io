Tourette Syndrome
Association of Australia

Logo & Webpage Design

The Tourette Syndrome Association of Australia stated that the original version of the webpage and logo design style make people associate with medicine and medical care. In 2019, it cooperated with BillyBlue design college to redesign the brand, hoping to present a more lively and warm style.

The logo is referenced the human body shape which same as the original logo and is added the acronyms of Tourette Syndrome which are the letters T and S as the main elements.

In web design, focus on accessibility. Using pictures and icons to replace text descriptions for better browsing, especially for users who are illiterate or use different languages.

Brisbane China Town

Logo design &
Typegraphy systems

A Chinatown is an ethnic enclave of Chinese people located outside mainland China, Hong Kong, Macau or Taiwan, most often in an urban setting. It can be an interesting and multicultural existence. The orientation of this design is to combine the Chinese culture element (calligraphy) and English letter, creating a multicultural visual style. And reference to the fish statue which is the main statue in the Brisbane Chinatown to design the secondary language.
